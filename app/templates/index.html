Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?
Yeah - totally free. No card linking, no subscriptions. Just upload a product and reviews - and get a report.
What exactly will I get after I analyze it?
You will receive a report with the main pains and pros of the product based on customer feedback. This will help you understand whether you should promote the product and what to improve.
Where does the feedback for analysis come from?
You can upload them manually or insert data collected from marketplaces (e.g. Amazon, Rozetka, Prom, etc.).
Do I need to understand AI or analytics?
No. Everything is organized simply: you add a product, upload reviews, choose a ready-made promt - and get the result in an understandable form.
Can multiple products be analyzed?
Yes, you can add several products to your personal cabinet and analyze them one by one. The first one is free of charge, further on according to the tariff.
Is it safe to upload testimonials?
Yes. All uploaded data is only used to generate the report. We do not share or publish your data.
How long does it take to analyze?
Typically, the report is generated in 1-2 minutes after the reviews are uploaded and the promt is selected.
